Help Page

Getting started:

  • From the login page, click "Create an Account" and fill in your information
  • Once created, fill in your credentials on the login page and click "Login"
  • From there, you will be directed to the Public Projects page, listing all projects currently active on the site

Using the Navbar

  • At the top of the screen on all logged-in pages there is a gray navigation bar with links to different sections of the site.
  • Public Projects: Takes you to the page listing all active projects on the site.
  • Username's Profile: Will say your username in place of "Username", takes you to version of your user-page, where you can edit your personal information that will be visible by others.
  • Your Projects: Filters the public projects page to show only projects you are involved with (as either admin or member)
  • Create Project: Form to create a new project and add it to the site
  • Help Page: Takes you back to this page.

Creating a project

  • To create a project, click "Create Project" at the top of the page inside the navigation bar
  • Fill in the requested information for your new project, then hit submit
  • Once submitted, you will be redirected to the Public Projects page, where your project will appear with other projects
  • To accept users into your project you must wait for them to request to join. Once they have, their name will appear in the list of members in the project with options to "Accept" or "Reject" the user from entering the project. If they are accepted, a line saying so will replace the options next to their name. If they are rejected, they will be removed from the list of members in the project.

Joining other's projects

  • To join another user's project, you must go to the project's page by clicking it's name from either the public project page or from the my projects page
  • From the project's individual page, if there is space available in the project you can click "Join" to request to join a project
  • Once you request to join, you must wait until the project admin goes into the project's page to accept or reject your request
  • If you are rejected, you will be removed from the list of members in the project
